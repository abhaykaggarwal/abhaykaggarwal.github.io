“I do believe in the power of story. I believe that stories have an important role to play in the formation of human beings, that they can stimulate, amaze, and inspire their listeners.”

-Hayao Miyazaki

This website is an exploration of Studio Ghibli movies and their themes, attempting to explore the question: “What can you imagine?”. Inspired by JD Thompson’s video on Hayao Miyazaki’s work, you can learn a little bit more about 2 of Miyazaki’s most famous movies.
